"WIPO Match: financing innovation to leverage IP for development

In the age of global knowledge economy, information overload and limited time, how can countries, organizations and innovators find the correct partners to help finance their innovation initiatives? Serving as a facilitator of cooperation, WIPO Match is a free online tool that can reduce time and effort in searching for appropriate partners to initiate, execute and accelerate innovation-driven projects, with intellectual property management as a core element of success."

— Compiled by Mary Hayrapetyan
